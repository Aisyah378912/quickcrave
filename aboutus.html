Company Background

Quick Crave and our initial (QC) is a modern fast food business created to cater to a community that craves delicious, fast and high-quality food.

We combine popular recipes that are loved by many—from juicy burgers to mouth-watering traditional Nasi Lemak dishes—to ensure that every dish we prepare is always exciting, fresh and satisfying.

Our History

Starting as a small kitchen with a Food Truck concept in 21 st January 2020, Quick Crave was founded by a group of four young entrepreneurs who saw a gap in the local fast food delivery ecosystem.

By focusing on the quality of raw materials and speed of preparation, our brand is now growing rapidly in the Shah Alam area and has become one of the top choices for Malaysians who crave 'Comfort Food' dishes at any time.
